Can one model replace an entire voice pipeline?

We built the same English voice assistant two ways and compared them over real phone calls via Plivo. One uses three specialized models. The other uses one.

Deepgram + Gemini + TTS vs Gemini 3.1 Flash Live Plivo Telephony
Findings
CategoryCascadedNative S2SNotes
Voice Quality Depends on TTS Natural Gemini Live's voice felt conversational on the phone. The cascaded approach sounds as good as your TTS provider, which varies.
Conversational Flow Good Very natural Both maintained context across turns. Gemini Live handled turn-taking more smoothly since it processes audio natively.
Multilingual Per-language 200+ languages Cascaded needs separate STT/TTS per language. Gemini Live auto-detects and responds in the caller's language.
Tonal Understanding No Yes STT converts speech to text, losing tone. Gemini Live hears how something is said, not just what.
Integration Complexity 3 APIs 1 API Three keys, three SDKs, audio conversion between stages vs one WebSocket connection.
Component Swappability Yes No Cascaded lets you swap STT, LLM, or TTS independently. Native is one model.
SynthID Watermarking No Yes Gemini Live watermarks all generated audio for AI content detection.
Vendor Lock-in / Control You own the stack Tied to Google Cascaded lets you negotiate, swap providers, and absorb pricing or terms changes from any one vendor. Native S2S means one platform owns your voice agent end to end.
Latency 3 sequential hops 1 WebSocket Each cascaded turn waits on STT, then LLM, then TTS in series. Gemini Live keeps a persistent WebSocket and starts generating voice while still processing input.
Failure Isolation Per-stage All-or-nothing If Deepgram has an outage you can swap STT and keep running. If Gemini Live goes down the entire agent is down with no fallback.
Cost Predictability Per-stage pricing Bundled per-minute Cascaded pricing is granular per provider, easier to forecast and optimize. Live API bundles everything into one per-minute meter, harder to model at scale.

Inherently Multilingual

Tonal Understanding

Understands not just what you say, but how you say it. Frustration, excitement, confusion are part of the input. Cascaded pipelines lose all tonal information at the STT stage.

Trust & Safety

SynthID Watermarking

All generated audio is imperceptibly watermarked with SynthID for reliable detection of AI-generated content.

Native

Native Voice Generation

Voice output is generated as part of the reasoning process. Tone, pacing, and emphasis match the intent naturally rather than being added by a separate TTS engine.

Gemini 3.1 Flash Live · English Conversation

Charon voice over Plivo telephony
Hello! It's great to hear from you. How can I help you today?
Hey, I want to understand basketball rules.
Sure! Basically, two teams try to score by throwing the ball into a hoop. You move the ball by dribbling or passing, and there are rules against things like running without dribbling or being overly physical. Is there a specific rule you had in mind?
Which is the best team in NBA right now?
I can't say for sure, as the "best" team can change quickly based on recent games and standings. I don't track current sports scores. You might want to check a sports news website for the most up-to-date information.
That will be it. Thank you so much for your help.
You're welcome! Have a great day.
When asked about current NBA standings, the agent correctly declined rather than guessing. The system prompt instructs it not to make up facts about current events.
